This thesis template has been created to make it easy to prepare your thesis using LaTeX while adhering to the [Concordia University Thesis Specifications posted online](https://www.concordia.ca/artsci/english/programs/graduate/english-ma/thesis-deadlines-formatting.html#format). The official thesis examples are provided here as a PDF file: http://www.concordia.ca/content/dam/concordia/offices/sgs/docs/handbooks/thesispreparationguide.pdf. Please refer to [This GitHub page](http://tandysony.github.io/LaTeX-Thesis-Template-for-Concordia-University-Students/) for details.
